    Three way miniature Push Button valve, normally closed, spring return with 10-32 UNF ports. All TAC Push Button valves feature a work hardened SST button plunger above a 15/32-32 threaded collar. Collar feature permits acceptance of a variety of TAC Air Pilot Operators (sold separately - see Related Products below), converting push button valve to an Air Piloted Valve. Button bottoms out on collar to prevent valve overstroke from Air Operator. Compact. Rugged. Made in the USA.
